About

The To-Do List Application is designed to help users keep track of their tasks and manage their time effectively. Whether you're organizing your daily schedule, planning long-term projects, or simply making a grocery list, this application provides the tools you need to stay on top of your tasks.

Features:

  • Create new tasks
  • View existing tasks
  • Update tasks
  • Delete tasks
  • Mark tasks as completed
